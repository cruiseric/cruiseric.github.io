About Me

Hello! My name is Eric Chan, and I am an aspiring web developer / software engineer. I was previously in hospitality and food services, and although I enjoyed doing what I did, I wanted a career that was more more stable both financially and in job security, mentally challenging and rewarding, and with room for growth. I got into programming because of a lifelong interest in technology, puzzles, and problem solving. I would love to bring my experience in hospitality to tech.

I enjoy learning new things and challenging myself to improve. I like to learn how to do things the "right way" but then be able to play around to learn why and how things work they way they do, and see if I can come up with other ways to do things. I believe I have strong analytical and critical thinking skills, and would love to demonstrate my curiosity and growth mindset.

I am an aspiring web developer, but there are many things I want to get better at. I want to learn how to play guitar and become fluent in Japanese. I want to get better at bouldering. I want to sky dive and scuba dive. I like trying new things!
